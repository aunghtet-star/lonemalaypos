## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

**"I can't see my table's order!"**
- Check the table button - are you on the right one?
- Look at the quick-switch bar for all active tables

**"My order disappeared!"**
- Did you complete checkout? Orders clear after payment
- Check if someone else cleared the table
- Refresh the page - orders should restore

**"Too many tables open!"**
- Focus on completing orders as payments come in
- Use "Clear All" at end of shift (with confirmation)
- Individual tables clear automatically after checkout

**"Stock says out but we have items"**
- Check if other tables have the item in their carts
- Stock is reserved across all pending orders
- Complete some orders to free up stock
